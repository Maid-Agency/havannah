Legal Requirements

All maid agencies in Malaysia must be licensed by the Ministry of Human Resources and comply with Immigration Department guidelines. Employers should ensure all maids have valid work permits and contracts, especially when hiring through a Filipino maid agency.

Cost of Hiring a Maid

The average cost of hiring through a maid agency in Malaysia ranges between RM10,000 and RM15,000. This includes processing fees, medical exams, visas, and airfare.

Conclusion

Choosing a reliable maid agency or Filipino maid agency ensures that your household runs smoothly with trained, legal, and well-supported domestic helpers. Always verify the agency's license and reputation before committing.
